Ash Guard Juice

Ash guard juice is said to work like a scrubber that scrubs all wastes inside your body. It is also a pranic vegetable that's loaded with energy.

Ingredients needed

  • 250g of ash guard
  • Pepper powder
  • Rock salt / table salt

How to prepare

  1. Peel the skin and remove the seeds from the ash guard piece.
  2. Add a pinch of salt and pepper powder for taste.
  3. Blend these using a mixer until it becomes a paste.
  4. Use a cloth or strainer to strain out the juice from the paste and consume it within 15 min
